**React-Agenda** is a simple agenda component for React.

<div className="subheading">Features overview</div>

<ul>
  <li><em>Multiple</em> views: day, week or custom range of days.</li>
  <li><em>Timeslot</em>: from one minute to sixty.</li>
  <li><em>Events</em>: shows events in the agenda based on a day and a time range.</li>
  <li><em>Resources</em>: enables separate lanes in the agenda (e.g.: rooms, collaborators, etc..)</li>
  <li><em>Actions callback</em>: click on timeslot, event or change date event.</li>
  <li><em>Locale</em>: dates format based on locale.</li>
  <li><em>Timezone</em>: converts date and time in selected timezone.</li>
</ul>

<div className="subheading">Possible future steps</div>

<ul>
  <li><em>Overlap events</em>: manage events with the same day and time that overlaps.</li>
  <li><em>Multi-days event</em>: manage events that fits multiple days.</li>
  <li><em>Drag&Drop</em>: move event card from one timeslot or day to another. Adjust time length.</li>
  <li><em>Color for resource</em>: Display different color of the card, header or day column based on the resource.</li>
